Most people send the same resume to every job. ATS systems reject 75% of resumes before
a human ever sees them. The ones that make it through often don't speak the language of
the specific role.

Manually customizing a resume for every application takes 30–60 minutes and most people

### 1. Build your profile once (5 minutes)
Write about your experience, projects, and skills in plain English. No special formatting
required — just describe what you've done. Our AI extracts and structures everything.

### 2. Paste any job description (30 seconds)
Drop in the job posting text. We extract the requirements, keywords, responsibilities, and
what really matters for that specific role.

### 3. Get scored, tailored resumes (2–3 minutes)
Receive 3 resume variations, each with a different strategic angle:
- **Technical Depth** — leads with your technical skills and stack
- **Impact & Results** — leads with quantified business outcomes
- **Leadership & Collaboration** — highlights team work and cross-functional impact

Each resume is scored on 4 dimensions, then automatically refined based on the scores.

### 4. Download and apply
One click for PDF or DOCX. Pick the variant that scores highest and submit with confidence.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the resume content 100% based on my profile?**
Yes. The AI only works with information you provide. It reshapes, emphasizes, and optimizes
your existing experience — it never invents credentials or fabricates accomplishments.

**Will employers know I used AI?**
No. The output is a standard resume document. There are no AI watermarks or disclosures
included in the downloaded files.

**How long does generation take?**
Typically 1–3 minutes depending on profile complexity and number of variants. The page
updates automatically when your resumes are ready.

**What file formats are supported?**
PDF and DOCX. Both are formatted for single-page (or appropriate length) professional resumes.

**Can I edit the generated resume?**
Download the DOCX and edit it in Word, Google Docs, or any editor. We recommend downloading
DOCX if you want to make manual adjustments.

**What if my score is low?**
The score breakdown shows exactly what's missing and why. Low scores often mean keywords
from the job description aren't present — you can update your profile to add missing context,
then regenerate.
